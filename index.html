About Me

Engineer by Logic,
Builder by Nature

I'm a B.Tech Information Technology student at GH Raisoni College of Engineering and Management, with a sharp focus on building .NET backends and Angular SPAs that don't just work — they perform under pressure.

My toolkit centres on ASP.NET Core, C#, TypeScript, and Angular — an architecture stack that lets me own the full product from database schema to pixel-perfect UI. I think in SOLID principles, write with async/await, and ship with confidence.

Beyond the web, I build native Android applications with Firebase and MVVM — because the best solution isn't always a browser tab. When a hackathon calls, I answer: competing at the national level sharpens the instinct to solve real problems under real constraints.

I'm not interested in writing code that just passes tests. I'm interested in systems that scale, queries that execute in microseconds, and UIs that feel alive. That's the standard I hold myself to.
